# AI TestOps Audit — Pricing Tiers

## 1) Lite (Fast Diagnostic)
**Price:** ₹1.5L – ₹2.5L  
**Duration:** 5–7 business days

Includes:
- 1 product / 1 pipeline assessment
- 10-section maturity scoring
- Top 10 risks + quick wins
- 60-minute executive readout

Best for:
- Teams needing a fast baseline before major changes

---

## 2) Standard (Recommended)
**Price:** ₹3L – ₹6L  
**Duration:** 2 weeks

Includes:
- Multi-flow risk-tiered audit
- CI/CD + automation reliability deep dive
- AI-in-testing control assessment
- 30-60-90 roadmap
- Executive report + technical appendix
- 2 readout sessions (leadership + engineering)

Best for:
- Mid-size teams preparing scaled AI TestOps rollout

---

## 3) Enterprise (Board-Ready)
**Price:** ₹8L – ₹15L  
**Duration:** 3–4 weeks

Includes:
- Multi-product / multi-team audit
- Compliance + auditability review
- Governance operating model + RACI
- KPI design + dashboard blueprint
- Release-confidence framework
- Leadership workshop + remediation plan

Best for:
- Large orgs needing decision-grade QA/TestOps governance

---

## Add-ons
- Jira + test data ingestion automation: ₹1L – ₹3L
- Weekly scorecard automation: ₹75K – ₹2L/month
- Fractional QA/TestOps leadership: ₹2L – ₹8L/month

## Sales Notes
- Lead with business outcomes, not tools.
- Convert Lite → Standard as default upsell.
- Convert Standard/Enterprise → 90-day implementation sprint.
